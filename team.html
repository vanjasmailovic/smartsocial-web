Vanja Smailović, PhD

Vanja Smailović is a Data Scientist. He successfully finished his PhD studies in 2016. He was a project leader of the ECASON project, within SmartSocial.


Darko Štriga, PhD

Darko Štriga is working at the Telecommunication Department of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing as a Research Associate; additionally, he was the Research Engineer at SmartSocial project.


Vedran Podobnik, PhD

Vedran Podobnik is an Assistant Professor at the Telecommunication Department of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing, University of Zagreb. He was the project leader of SmartSocial project, alongside Vanja.


Ignac Lovrek, PhD

Ignac Lovrek is with the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing of the University of Zagreb where he works as a professor at the Department of Telecommunications. He is an associate member of the Croatian Academy of Sciences and Arts. He was Head of the Department of Telecommunications (1991-1994), Vice Dean for science (1996-1998) and President of the Management Board of the Croatian Academic and Research Network CARNet (2002-2004). He is now Chairman of the Doctoral Study Committee and Deputy Chairman of the Supervisory Board of the company Ericsson Nikola Tesla.
